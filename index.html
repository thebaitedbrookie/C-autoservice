Honda Motor Company, Ltd., or simply Honda, is a Japanese auto manufacturer. The company also manufactures motorcycles, power equipment, aircraft, robots and mountain bikes. Their luxury vehicle division is Acura.

In 2003 Honda became the first Japanese automaker to be a net exporter from the United States. They exported 108,705 Honda and Acura models and they imported 88,357. Indeed, the globalization of the auto industry has made the distinction between American and foreign manufacturers more opaque.

For years the Honda Accord was known as the vehicle with the best resale value. That title now belongs to Subaru, although Honda still makes good cars.

Honda Hyrbid Repair

Crawford’s Auto can service hybrid vehicles as well as regular gasoline vehicles. Honda’s current hybrid vehicles include the Accord Hybrid, Clarity Plug-In Hybrid, Clarity Fuel Cell and CR-V hybrid. The main difference of hybrid vehicles is the battery and charging system. In a regular gasoline vehicle the battery and charging system are used to start the engine and power electronic components. In a hybrid vehicle they are used to power some of the drive of the vehicle.
